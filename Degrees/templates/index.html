Project 2: Thesis and Summary

Our team believes that the pursuit of a 4 year degree is becomming cost prohibitive. Using data retrieved from recent enrollment and tuition studies, we suspect that the number of students going into a 4 year degree program is beginning to drop across the United States and that factors, such as ballooning student debt and the presense of affordable alternatives, are driving a significant change to this long-term trend.
